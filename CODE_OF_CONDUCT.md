# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

We pledge to make participation welcoming and harassment-free regardless of age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, experience level, nationality, race, religion, sexual identity or orientation.

Use welcoming language, respect differing viewpoints, give constructive feedback, and focus on what helps learners. Harassment, insults, trolling, publishing private information, and sexualized language or imagery are not tolerated.

Report concerns privately to the repository maintainers through GitHub. Maintainers may remove content, issue warnings, or restrict participation. Adapted from the [Contributor Covenant 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

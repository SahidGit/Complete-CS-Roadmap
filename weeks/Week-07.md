# Week 07 — React Fundamentals

## Goal
Build component-based interfaces with clear data flow and accessible interactions.

## Learning Objectives
- Compose reusable components with props and state.
- Use events, effects and hooks correctly.
- Choose controlled forms and lift state only as far as necessary.

## Topics and Concepts
JSX, components, props, state, events, conditional rendering, lists and keys, controlled inputs, effects, custom hooks and accessibility in single-page applications.

## Resources
- **Official documentation:** [React Learn](https://react.dev/learn), [React accessibility](https://react.dev/learn/accessibility).
- **Course/video:** [React tutorial](https://react.dev/learn/tutorial-tic-tac-toe), [Academind](https://www.youtube.com/@academind).

## Interview Questions
1. What is the difference between props and state?
2. Why are stable keys important in lists?
3. When should an effect be avoided?

## Mini Projects
1. **Notes App** — intermediate, 12h. Create, edit and search notes. *Skills:* controlled inputs. *Extension:* add persistence.
2. **Dashboard** — intermediate, 12h. Data cards and filters. *Skills:* component composition. *Extension:* add accessible charts.
3. **Blog UI** — intermediate, 10h. Article cards and detail view. *Skills:* props and layouts. *Extension:* add reading progress.

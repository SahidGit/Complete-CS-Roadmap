## 🎯 Study Strategy

To master DSA efficiently, follow this pattern:
1. **Understand Primitives First**: Do not skip to Graphs before mastering Linked Lists and Trees.
2. **Focus on Patterns, Not Problems**: Instead of memorizing 1,000 LeetCode problems, understand the 15 core patterns (Sliding Window, Two Pointers, DFS/BFS, Backtracking).
3. **Verify Complexity**: Always analyze the Time and Space complexity of your solution using Big O analysis.
